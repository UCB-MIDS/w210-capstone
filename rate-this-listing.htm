Airbnb Listing Rating Prediction

INTRODUCTION

THE PROBLEM

AIRBNB: Can we reject the unqualified hosts or properties even before they receive negative or bad reviews? Can we use predicted renter ratings as a mechanism to show personalized rental recommendations to customers?

​

CUSTOMERS: Should I renovate my place first? What amenities do customers care the most? Will customers be satisfied with what I can provide? Can certain amenities earn me a higher rating?
 

THE CHALLENGE

Although the original dataset is very rich, informative and realistic, it has hundreds of data fields including both numerical and textual data and requires heavy cleaning and wrangling.

Among these data fields, it's quite a chanllenge to tell which features are relevant and helpful to the prediction. On the other hand, we also need to be wise in the modeling selection.

THE SOLUTION

Our ultimate goal is to build a generic model for predicting customer ratings using Airbnb data, and then use transfer learning to apply this model to other datasets like Yelp's restaurant review
Airbnb can screen and filter the properties before they are on market, so they can give suggestions or even reject poor-quality properties listed on Airbnb based on the evaluation.
Perspective hosts can use our tool to improve their properties before they are listed on market and get bad reviews.
